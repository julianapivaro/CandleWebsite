Welcome to CANDLE BURNING

"Happiness can be found even in the darkest of times, if one only remembers to turn on the light."

Where light meets craftsmanship. We are a passionate candle company dedicated to creating unique, hand-poured candles that bring warmth, ambiance, and a touch of luxury to every space.

We believe that candles are more than just a source of light—they are an experience. Each of our products is carefully crafted to help you relax, unwind, and create an atmosphere that reflects your personal style.

Our Craft

At Candle Burning, candle-making is both an art and a science. We meticulously hand-pour each candle, using only the finest waxes, lead-free wicks, and carefully curated fragrances. Every step of the process is done with love and attention to detail, ensuring a superior product that not only looks beautiful but also provides a long-lasting and clean burn.

Our waxes are sustainably sourced, and we offer a range of options, including soy wax, beeswax, and paraffin blends, each chosen for its ability to hold and release scent effectively while burning cleanly. Our fragrance oils are high-quality and phthalate-free, so you can enjoy the perfect balance of scent without any harmful chemicals.

The result is a candle that is more than just a simple decorative item—it’s a piece of our story, carefully crafted to bring light and luxury into your life. Whether you're looking to add a cozy touch to your living room, create a calming atmosphere in your bathroom, or find the perfect gift, our candles are made with you in mind.

Our History

Candle Burning was founded in 2019, born out of a desire to share our love for candles with the world. From humble beginnings, we’ve steadily grown, refining our techniques and expanding our offerings to create a diverse range of scents, sizes, and designs that suit every occasion.

As we perfected our craft, word began to spread about our unique, handcrafted candles, and we soon found ourselves welcoming customers from all over. Today, our candles are cherished in homes, offices, and special events, each one symbolizing our unwavering dedication to quality and beauty.
